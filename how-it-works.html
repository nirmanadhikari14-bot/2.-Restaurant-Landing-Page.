How TrustRaksha Works

A simple, transparent, and consent-based process designed to protect your digital life — legally and ethically.

1

User Requests a Service

You start by submitting a service request through our website. You choose the type of security service you need and briefly describe your concern. No passwords, OTPs, or sensitive data are ever requested.

2

Written Consent & Scope Definition

Before any security check begins, we take your written consent. The scope of work is clearly defined so you know exactly what will be reviewed and what will not. This ensures full legal and ethical compliance.

3

Security Check & Expert Guidance

Our team performs a safe and ethical security review based on the agreed scope. This may include audits, configuration checks, or risk analysis — always with your involvement and transparency.

4

Report & Recommendations

You receive a clear, easy-to-understand report explaining identified risks, their impact, and practical recommendations to improve your digital security.

5

Post-Service Support

We don’t disappear after the report. TrustRaksha provides post-service guidance and answers your questions to help you implement fixes and maintain long-term digital safety.

Our Commitment to Legal & Ethical Security

TrustRaksha follows a strict no-password, no-OTP policy. All services are performed only with client consent, within defined boundaries, and with complete respect for privacy and data protection laws.
